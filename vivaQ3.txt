Viva Questions and Answers
1. What is descriptive statistics?

It summarizes dataset using statistical measures.

2. What is mean?

Average value of dataset.

3. What is median?

Middle value after sorting data.

4. What is standard deviation?

Measure of data variation.

5. Why use groupby()?

To group records by categorical values.

6. What is categorical variable?

Variable containing labels or categories.

7. What is quantitative variable?

Variable containing numeric values.

8. What does describe() return?

Count, mean, std, min, max and percentiles.

9. What is percentile?

Percentage position of value in dataset.

10. What is Iris dataset?

Dataset used for classification and statistics.

11. Name the Iris species used.

Iris-setosa, Iris-versicolor, Iris-virginica.

12. Which library is used?

Pandas.

13. What did you observe in this practical?

Observed statistical analysis on grouped and Iris dataset data.

14. Why is standard deviation important?

It shows spread of data.

15. What is the use of filtering?

To extract records matching condition.
