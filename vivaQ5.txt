1. What is Logistic Regression?

6. What is confusion matrix?

7. What is TP?

True Positive.

8. What is TN?

True Negative.

9. What is FP?

False Positive.

10. What is FN?

False Negative.

11. Formula of Accuracy?

(TP + TN) / Total

12. Formula of Precision?

TP / (TP + FP)

13. Formula of Recall?

TP / (TP + FN)

14. What is Error Rate?

1 - Accuracy
