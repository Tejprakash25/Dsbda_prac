Viva Questions and Answers
1. What is Text Analytics?

Process of extracting information from text data.

2. What is Tokenization?

Breaking text into words or tokens.

3. What is POS Tagging?

Identifying grammatical category of words.

4. What are stop words?

Common words with little meaning.

5. Why remove stop words?

To improve text processing efficiency.

6. What is Stemming?

Reducing words to root form.

7. What is Lemmatization?

Converting words into meaningful base form.

8. Difference between stemming and lemmatization?

Lemmatization gives meaningful words while stemming may not.

9. What is TF?

Term Frequency of a word in document.

10. What is IDF?

Inverse Document Frequency.

11. What is TF-IDF?

Importance score of words in documents.

12. Which library is used for NLP?

NLTK

13. Which library is used for TF-IDF?

scikit-learn

14. What did you observe in this practical?

Observed preprocessing and TF-IDF representation of text.

15. Why is text preprocessing important?

To clean and prepare text for analysis.
